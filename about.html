About Me

Oh wow how I have grown!

The past six months have been life-changing. I decided to take a leap of faith and pursue a career in tech. Everything about the tech industry piques my interest and curiosity.

During my time at the UNH Coding Boot Camp I learned at an extremely fast pace. It was challenging but rewarding. I learned so much from my teachers and fellow classmates. From being able to problem solve, collaborate, and learn to feel empowered as a woman in technology. This new identity is particularly important to me as I continue to pursue professional development at women in technology conferences.

I have two beautiful children who inspire me daily to work harder, learn more, and pursue my future in technology. It is a very exciting journey! We also own a beautiful dog named Tessa who is a rescue and joy to spend time with on our downtime.
